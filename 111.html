1366 + 1402 Server Rules

Read and Follow The Server Rules Provided Below!

There must be a level of respect when commenting on World Chat. Friendly banter is okay, but attacks on a person’s race, nationality, and/or orientation are unacceptable. Slander towards an alliance, personal attacks (regardless if the statements are true, half-true, or elaborations), and unsubstantiated accusations will not be tolerated.
Server will designate players with a KoS (Kill on Sight) designation. If a player from one alliance attacks/scouts another alliance member, the attacking player will be declared KoS (Kill on Sight) immediately. If a player without an alliance attacks/scouts a player in an alliance, the attacking player will be messaged to stop immediately. If this behavior continues, the player will be declared KoS.
Subordinate cities are always protected under the NAP.
  • For a player to keep a newly released subordinate city, it must be either unlocked or moved to within 20 minutes.
  • Once unlocked, it cannot be attacked. If the sub is locked after the first 20 minutes, the sub can be taken by the first player who can break the lock.
  • If a player accidentally captures a sub belonging to another player, the sub must be released immediately. The captor will not be attacked unless the sub is not released within a timely manner.
  • If a player is zeroed, their subs can be captured. They must be released once the player is out of bubble and online. If the subs are not released in a timely manner, the Server Council will convene regarding the behavior, then designate and enforce punishment.
    • Server Council will make and enforce server rules and designate punishment for players who break server rules. The ruling King/Queen is the highest authority and has the final say in all Server Council decisions. The Server Council consists of R5s from top alliances.
  • KoS status means that the player is free to be attacked at any time without consequence.
  • Players who help a KoS player will also be declared KoS.
  • KoS status will remain in effect until the ruling King/Queen determines otherwise.
    • If a KoS player is in an alliance, the alliance R5 will be given 24 hours to expel them. If the player is not removed from the alliance, the entire alliance will become KoS.
    • The Server Council will vote monthly regarding KoS status.
    • The ruling King/Queen will send out the final verdict.

    Subordinate cities are vital assets in Evony TKR. They provide numerous benefits including troop training boosts, resource production, and additional buffs during combat. Protect your subs and follow server rules to ensure a harmonious community.
