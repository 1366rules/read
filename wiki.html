Getting Started

Beginner's Guide

Welcome to 1366 Evony! This guide will help you establish your empire and grow your power efficiently.

Quick Tips

  • Complete the tutorial for valuable rewards
  • Upgrade your City Hall to unlock new features
  • Join an alliance early for protection
  • Focus on resource production in the first few days
  • Use shields when offline to protect your city

Resources

Resources are the lifeblood of your empire. Manage them wisely to ensure steady growth.

Food

Used to feed your troops and research certain technologies

  • Production: Farms
  • Storage: Granary
  • Base Rate: 500/hr

Wood

Essential for building construction and troop training

  • Production: Lumber Mills
  • Storage: Warehouse
  • Base Rate: 500/hr

Stone

Required for advanced buildings and fortifications

  • Production: Quarries
  • Storage: Warehouse
  • Base Rate: 300/hr

Iron

Needed for military equipment and advanced research

  • Production: Iron Mines
  • Storage: Warehouse
  • Base Rate: 300/hr

Troop Types

Build a balanced army to handle any military situation. Each unit type has strengths and weaknesses.

Infantry Units

Strong against cavalry but vulnerable to archers. Best used for defense and close combat.
